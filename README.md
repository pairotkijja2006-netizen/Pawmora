# 🐾 Pawmora

A TikTok-style video platform designed to make pet adoption more engaging, accessible, and personalized by connecting users with adoptable animals through an interactive scrolling experience.

## 💡 Inspiration
Traditional pet adoption platforms often present animals as static listings, making it difficult for users to form emotional connections.

Pawmora reimagines this experience by making pet discovery more interactive and engaging — similar to how users naturally consume content today.

Instead of treating pets like listings, the platform helps users build emotional connections and encourages more successful adoptions.

---

## 🚀 What It Does
Pawmora is a TikTok-style video feed application designed specifically for pet lovers.

Users can:
- Scroll through an endless feed of adoptable pets  
- Save their favorites  
- Engage with the community through likes and comments  

The platform also helps shelters showcase pets to a wider audience, increasing their chances of adoption.

---

## 🎮 Features

- **Vertical Video Feed**  
  Immersive, full-screen portrait experience optimized for mobile and desktop  

- **Interactive Engagement**
  - 🐾 **Paw Likes**: Custom paw-print like button  
  - 💬 **Comments**: Toggleable comment section  
  - 🔖 **Saves**: Bookmark pets to your collection  

- **Canadian Content**  
  Curated pets from across Canada (Toronto, Vancouver, Montreal, etc.)  

- **User Authentication**  
  Secure sign-up and login powered by Supabase

---

## 🧠 Why Pawmora?
Instead of treating pets like static listings, Pawmora creates a more natural and engaging discovery experience.

By combining short-form video with interactive features, the platform:
- Builds stronger emotional connections  
- Improves pet-owner matching  
- Encourages more successful adoptions
